Privacy Policy Introduction

Welcome to LinsPlay. This Privacy Policy outlines the policies and procedures of LINS IO regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our Android application, LinsPlay. LINS IO respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

LinsPlay is designed as an offline HTML5 game collection. Because of the nature of the application, our data practices are highly minimized. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how it is treated.

Information We Collect

LinsPlay itself does NOT directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information to LINS IO.

Because LinsPlay functions primarily as an offline HTML5 game application with no user account system, server database, or cloud infrastructure maintained by us, there is no direct mechanism or intent for LINS IO to harvest user data. However, the application integrates third-party services—specifically Google AdMob and Google Play Services—that automatically collect certain technical and usage information required for their operation. This data collection is handled entirely by Google according to Google's own Privacy Policy and is outside the direct control of LINS IO.

Information We Do Not Collect

To provide complete transparency, we explicitly confirm that LINS IO does not collect, process, or store any of the following categories of data:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Names, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, or dates of birth.
  • Authentication Data: Usernames, passwords, or security PINs, as there is no login, signup, or Firebase Authentication implemented.
  • Financial Information: Credit card details, banking information, or billing records, as there are no in-app purchases or payment systems.
  • User-Generated Content: Messages, chat logs, social media profiles, or gameplay screenshots, as there are no chat or social features.
  • Contact Lists: Device address books or contact lists.
  • Media Files: Photos, videos, or audio recordings stored on your device.

Device Information

While we do not collect this data directly, Google AdMob and Google Play Services may automatically gather device-specific information to ensure proper ad rendering, app stability, and analytics. This information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Device Model and Manufacturer
  • Operating System version
  • Device Identifiers
  • Language and Regional settings
  • Diagnostic and Crash Information (if applicable through Google services)

This information is processed by Google and is not accessible in a personally identifiable format by LINS IO.

Advertising ID

To provide advertisements within the App, Google AdMob utilizes the Android Advertising ID (AAID). The AAID is a unique, user-resettable identifier for advertising provided by Google Play services. It gives users better controls and provides developers with a standard system to continue to monetize their apps securely.

Google AdMob uses this identifier to serve targeted advertisements based on your interests and to measure the performance of advertising campaigns. You have the ability to reset this identifier or opt-out of personalized ads entirely through your device's settings.

IP Address

When the App connects to the internet to request an advertisement, your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address is automatically transmitted to Google AdMob servers. The IP address is a numerical label assigned to each device connected to a computer network. Google utilizes this IP address to deliver the advertisement payload back to your device, prevent fraud, and infer broad geographic distribution.

Approximate Location Information

LinsPlay does not request precise GPS location permissions (such as ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION or ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION). However, approximate location information may be deduced by Google AdMob using your device's IP address. This inferred, approximate location data (typically at the city or regional level) is processed strictly when required by Google AdMob to serve geographically relevant advertisements and comply with local advertising regulations.

Advertising and Google AdMob

LinsPlay relies on Google AdMob as its sole method of monetization. Consequently, while the HTML5 games themselves are playable offline, an internet connection is utilized when available specifically for loading and displaying advertisements served by Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may automatically collect information including, but not limited to, Advertising Performance Data, Network Information, and Device Identifiers to provide, evaluate, and improve its advertising services. The practices of Google AdMob are governed by Google's Privacy Policy, and LINS IO does not control the tracking technologies employed by these advertising networks.

Google Play Services

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store and operates within the Android ecosystem, which intrinsically relies on Google Play Services. Google Play Services may run in the background and collect baseline operational data, crash logs, and usage metrics to ensure device security, app integrity, and reliable performance. This data collection is systemic to the Android operating system and is governed by Google's terms and privacy agreements.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Because LinsPlay is a native Android application featuring HTML5 games, it does not use traditional web cookies in the way a standard web browser does. However, the integrated third-party SDKs (such as Google AdMob) may utilize mobile identifiers, local storage, and similar tracking technologies to function correctly, track ad views, frequency cap advertisements, and prevent fraudulent activity.

Internet Connection

The application is designed as an offline HTML5 game collection, meaning the core gameplay mechanics do not require an active internet connection to function. Internet is required only for loading advertisements served by Google AdMob. If your device is disconnected from the internet, the games will continue to function normally, but advertisements will not be fetched or displayed during that offline period.

Permissions Used

To function as intended, specifically to support our advertising integration, LinsPlay requests the following minimal Android system permissions:

  • INTERNET: This permission allows the App to open network sockets. It is used exclusively by the Google AdMob SDK to request and download advertising payloads over the internet.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: This permission allows the App to access information about networks. It is used by the Google AdMob SDK to determine whether an active internet connection is available before attempting to request an advertisement, thereby saving device battery and data usage.

Why Certain Information May Be Processed

While LINS IO does not directly collect your data, the automated data collection performed by integrated third-party services (Google) is necessary for the following operational purposes:

  • Ad Serving: To deliver, manage, and display advertisements within the App.
  • Analytics and Performance: To understand how ads are performing and to generate aggregate statistics.
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent invalid clicks, malicious activity, and ad fraud.
  • App Stability: To monitor crash information (if applicable) ensuring the underlying Google services remain stable and reliable.
