Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LINS IO ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the LinsPlay Android application.

LinsPlay is an offline HTML5 gaming application. The games are designed to work without requiring users to create an account or submit personal information. Internet access is only required for loading advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Information We Collect

LINS IO does not directly collect or store personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Home Address
  • Password
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Messages
  • Payment Information

The application does not require user registration or login.

Information Processed by Google Services

To display advertisements, LinsPlay uses Google AdMob. Google and its services may automatically process certain technical information including but not limited to:

  • Device Model
  • Operating System Version
  • Android Advertising ID (AAID)
  • IP Address
  • Approximate Location
  • Language Settings
  • Country
  • Device Identifiers
  • Network Information
  • Advertising Performance Data
  • Diagnostic Information

This information is collected directly by Google according to Google's own privacy practices. LINS IO does not control or store this information.

Advertising

LinsPlay displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

Advertisements help support the continued development and maintenance of the application.

Google may use collected information to deliver relevant advertisements, measure advertising performance, detect fraud, improve services, and maintain platform security.

Internet Connection

The games included in LinsPlay are playable offline.

An internet connection is only required for loading advertisements and related Google advertising services.

Permissions

LinsPlay only requests permissions necessary for normal operation, including:

  • INTERNET
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

No unnecessary permissions are requested.

Children's Privacy

LinsPlay is intended for a general audience.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through our application, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

Data Security

We value user privacy. Since LinsPlay does not directly collect personal information, we do not maintain personal databases containing user data.

Google is responsible for protecting any information processed through its own services.

Third-Party Services

LinsPlay uses third-party services operated independently from LINS IO.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy
  • Google Terms

Please review their policies to understand how they collect and process information.

Your Rights

You have the right to review Google's privacy practices through the official policies listed above. Since LINS IO does not directly collect personal information, requests regarding Google-collected information should be directed to Google.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
